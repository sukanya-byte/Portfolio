Technical Valuation App

The Technical Valuation App enables field executives to inspect properties, document site conditions, capture evidence, and submit valuation reports directly from the field. Previously, the process relied on a web portal that wasn't designed for on-site inspections. The new mobile experience transformed a fragmented documentation process into a structured workflow built around how field executives actually work.

THE WORKFLOW

Five stages. One complete valuation report.

Instead of exposing users to thirteen individual forms, the experience was structured around five operational stages that reflect how inspections happen in reality.

Stage · 01 Discover

A homepage that explains, not advertises.

The old experience pushed products. The new one introduces borrowing journeys. Product categories, trust signals, branch presence, and key lending segments now appear before deep navigation begins.

WHAT CHANGED
  • Product discovery surfaced on the homepage
  • Trust metrics moved above the fold
  • Simplified product architecture
  • Faster access to loan categories
Stage · 02 Verify

Confirm the property matches the application.

At the site, users verify property details, addresses, ownership information, occupancy status, and construction conditions.

WHAT CHANGED
  • Primary Applicant Details
  • Property Details
  • Address Verification
  • Occupancy Details
  • Connectivity Details
Stage · 03 Qualify

Capture evidence from the field.

The application guides users through collecting photographs, measurements, physical observations, and construction information.

WHAT CHANGED
  • Property Photography
  • Construction Progress
  • Physical Verification
  • Site Observations
  • Supporting Evidence
Stage · 04 Evaluate

Evaluate and record valuation observations.

Users complete the technical assessment by documenting valuation inputs, deviations, builder information, and references.

Review once. Submit confidently.

Users review all completed information, resolve pending items, and submit the final valuation report.

DESIGN DECISIONS

We rebuilt the field workflow around it so the agent's hands stay on the case, never on the form.

Instead of recreating the web experience on mobile, the process was redesigned around how valuation executives perform inspections.

🏗 Architecture

Cloud-native vs offline-first?

A cloud-only app would have been simpler to build — and useless in a basement. We made the entire workflow operable with zero bars, syncing only when the agent's phone re-joins the network.

OFFLINE-FIRST ✓
📸 Capture UX

Camera then validate or validate then commit?

We chose live preflight. The shutter fires only after the framing is checked. This cost us 80 ms of latency and saved us 22% of photos from being rejected.

LIVE PREFLIGHT ✓
🌐 Language

English, or vernacular?

Half the agent base reads Hindi more comfortably than English. Vernacular toggles, voice-to-text for notes, and Hindi-friendly form labels were table stakes — not a phase 2.

EN + HI + 4 MORE ✓
